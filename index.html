Stim Box Controller

Use Chrome or Edge on a laptop.

Before you start
  1. Unplug your laptop from the wall (run on battery power).
  2. Open this page in Chrome or Edge.
  3. Use the provided USB cable to connect the Stim Box to your laptop.
  4. Put the two electrodes on the same forearm, 3–6 inches (8–15 cm) apart.
    Warning: Do not place electrodes on opposite arms, across the chest, neck, head, or in any way that could cross the heart. If you place electrodes across your body, you will be removed from the activity.
    Warning: Do not participate if you have any implanted electronic medical device (for example: pacemaker, ICD, neurostimulator, cochlear implant, insulin pump) or if you have metal implants near where the electrodes would go. If you are unsure, stop and ask the instructor before connecting electrodes or turning on the stim box.
  5. Connect the 12V battery and switch the battery to “|” (ON).
  6. I confirm that I have connected the electrodes, and am running on battery power as described above.
  7. Press Connect, select the device in the pop-up, and wait until it says Connected.
  8. Now turn ON the power switch on the Stim Box.
